Projects & Events

Short overall description about Project and Events perfomed

The third Pan African Youth Camp

The third Pan African Youth Camp, prepared and organised by TYC in coraboration with main guests NEEC and NGO Tanzania. The camp comprised of more than 50 Tanzanian Youths with different start ups and ideas. We were trained on how to implement and monetise our ideas, but also how to lead our organisations for the benefit of singular person to the whole community. The street writers, we were fortunate to be one among the organisations selected for the training and showcasing our activities

Education Seminar for Seecondary Students

In collaboration with Teacher Sam Education Centre, The street writers we had a chance to conduct a seminar with secondary students available at the centre. The talk was about time management, leadership and how they can give back to the community while being students. We also had a chance to introduce writing programs available at our organisation but also invite ideas from the students on how we can reach them further and connect with them. We also asked the challenges that they are facing as students and together we discussed the solutions.
